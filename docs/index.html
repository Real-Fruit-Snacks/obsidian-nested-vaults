Obsidian Nested Vaults

Magically scope your entire Obsidian vault down to a specific folder. Focus on what matters.

🔍

Absolute Focus

Instantly hides all folders, files, and tags outside your active sub-vault. It's like having a dedicated workspace without the hassle of multiple vaults.

🛡️

Aggressive Blocking

Prevents accidental navigation. If you click a link that leads outside your scoped sub-vault, the plugin blocks it and slams the tab shut.

⚡

Native Integration

Deeply integrated into Obsidian. Core Search, Graph View, and Backlinks are all automatically filtered to respect your boundaries.

Zero Friction Workflow

1

Right-click any folder and select Set as Active Sub-Vault.

2

Watch as your entire Obsidian interface instantly collapses down to only show that folder.

3

Click the Leave Sub-Vault button in your ribbon or status bar to return home.
